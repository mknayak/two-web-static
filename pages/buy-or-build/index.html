Avoid Common Pitfalls

Build: Full Control, Higher Investment

Building your solution enables tailored customization, scalable architecture, and deep integration, but requires significant time, skilled talent, and maintenance effort.

Buy: Faster Go-to-Market, Less Ownership

Buying a SaaS or third-party solution gets you up and running fast, often with ongoing support and compliance benefits. However, you may be constrained by limited customization and vendor lock-in.

Hybrid: Customize the Best of Both Worlds

Sometimes, the right approach is blending off-the-shelf platforms with custom modules. Extend, integrate, or wrap to fit your needs without starting from scratch.
